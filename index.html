What is Yotonix?

Yotonix is an annual performance held jointly by Revolution Chinese Yoyo and Photonix at the University of Michigan.


The name: "Yotonix" comes from combining "Yoyo" and "Photonix"


Yotonix was started in the spring of 2017. Each year, it features unique choreographies from Revolution and Photonix not performed at any other performances on campus. The show also includes a collaboration set with both Revolution and Photonix members, as well as a special Glowsuit set. Finally, a multitude of guest performers are invited each year.
